You are a senior full-stack engineer and system architect.

Your task is to design and implement a lightweight ERP-style internal data management dashboard. The system will replace a manual workflow currently managed through Word and Excel documents.

The goal is to build a structured, scalable, and professional system focused on operational data entry, analytics, and reporting.

SYSTEM OBJECTIVE

Create a cloud-based internal dashboard application that allows structured record management, automated calculations, analytics dashboards, and detailed reporting.

The system must be lightweight, fast, and usable on desktop and mobile devices.
